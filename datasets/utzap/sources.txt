1. Introduction

In this document we describe how to prepare the UT-Zappos50K images for
use with demo3.py.

Download the images from
http://vision.cs.utexas.edu/projects/finegrained/utzap50k/.

Place them so that "images/utzap/Shoes/Boat Shoes/ALDO/8045627.325.jpg"
is a valid path.
